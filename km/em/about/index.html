Our Mission

We wish to make disciples of all nations and bring glory to God.

We Value All People

(Genesis 1:26) - Then God said, “Let us make mankind in our image, in our likeness...”

We Work to Make Everyone a Disciple of Jesus

(Colossians 1:28) - He is the one we proclaim, admonishing and teaching everyone with all wisdom...

We Want God to Be Glorified In All Things

(1 Corinthians 10:31) - So whether you eat or drink or whatever you do, do it all for the glory of God.
