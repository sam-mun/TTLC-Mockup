EM House Church

For those who want to have an intimate relationship with Jesus, a meaningful gathering of like-minded people who consider each other family is absolutely necessary.

What is a Home Church?

A home church comprises 8 to 12 young adults, regardless of age. Some house churches are made up of people of similar age, and some house churches are made up of people ranging from newlyweds to elderly couples in their 70s.

Youth Home Churches

For our teenagers, we understand the unique journey of faith you're on. That's why we've formed youth home churches, specifically designed to meet your spiritual needs and help you grow in your relationship with Jesus.

If you want to know more about House Church, please contact us via email: tucsontruelightchurch.com and leave your questions. Thank you.
